LynxApps/Duplicate Photo Finder/Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Your photos never leave your device. All scanning, comparison, and duplicate detection happens entirely on your device. We do not collect, upload, transmit, or sell your photos.

1. Overview

LynxApps ("we," "us," "our") operates the Duplicate Photo Finder application ("App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use the App. We are committed to protecting your privacy — your photos are processed only on your device using on-device AI technology.

2. Information We Collect

Photo & Video Data

The App accesses your photo library to detect duplicate and similar photos, analyze metadata (resolution, quality, format), and provide intelligent recommendations.

Important: All processing happens locally on your device. Your photos are never uploaded, transmitted, or stored on our servers.

Device & Usage Information

Through third-party services, we automatically collect:

  • Device model and operating system version
  • App usage data and performance metrics
  • Crash reports and error logs
  • Advertising identifier (for personalized ads)

Subscription & Payment Information

All in-app purchases are processed through Apple's App Store (iOS) or Google Play Billing (Android), depending on which version of the App you use. We do not collect or store payment card information. We receive only purchase confirmation, subscription status, and receipt information.

3. How We Use Information

  • App functionality: to detect and manage duplicate photos on your device
  • Service improvement: to enhance features and user experience
  • Analytics: to understand usage and improve performance
  • Support: to provide customer support and troubleshooting
  • Advertising: to deliver personalized ads (if enabled)
  • Legal compliance: to comply with applicable laws

5. Data Security

  • On-device processing ensures photos never leave your phone
  • Encryption in transit for analytics data
  • Regular security updates and patches
  • Limited server-side data retention

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Privacy Rights

General Users

  • Revoke photo library access anytime in your device's Settings (iOS Settings or Android App permissions)
  • Disable personalized ads in your device's Settings (iOS Settings or Android Ads settings)
  • Delete the app to stop data collection
  • Request information about data we process

European Union (GDPR)

  • Access: request a copy of your data
  • Deletion: request deletion of your data
  • Rectification: correct inaccurate data
  • Portability: receive your data in a portable format
  • Object: object to processing

California (CCPA/CPRA)

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete collected data
  • Right to opt-out of sale/sharing of personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13, and will promptly delete such information if discovered.

8. Data Retention

  • Photos: never stored on our servers
  • Usage analytics: retained up to 60 days
  • Crash reports: retained up to 90 days
  • Ad data: governed by Google's retention policies

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We'll reflect changes by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
