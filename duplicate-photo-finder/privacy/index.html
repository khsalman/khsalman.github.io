Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Privacy First Commitment: Your photos never leave your device. All scanning and duplicate detection happens entirely on your device. We do not collect, upload, transmit, or sell your photos.

1. Overview

LynxApps ("Company," "we," or "us") operates the Duplicate Photo Finder application ("App"). This Privacy Policy explains our data practices and how we handle your information when you use our App.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Your photos remain private and are processed only on your device using on-device AI technology.

2. Information We Collect

Photo and Video Data

The App accesses your photo library to:

  • Detect duplicate and similar photos
  • Analyze photo metadata (resolution, quality, format)
  • Provide intelligent recommendations

Important: All processing happens locally on your device. Your photos are never uploaded, transmitted, or stored on our servers.

Device and Usage Information

Through third-party services, we automatically collect:

  • Device model and operating system version
  • App usage data and performance metrics
  • Crash reports and error logs
  • Advertising identifier (for personalized ads)
  • App performance and feature usage analytics

Subscription and Payment Information

All in-app purchases are processed through Apple's App Store. We do not collect or store payment card information. Apple processes your subscription and we receive only:

  • Purchase confirmation
  • Subscription status
  • Receipt information

3. How We Use Information

  • App Functionality: To detect and manage duplicate photos on your device
  • Service Improvement: To enhance features and user experience
  • Analytics: To understand how you use the app and improve performance
  • Support: To provide customer support and troubleshooting
  • Advertising: To deliver personalized ads (if enabled)
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • On-device processing ensures photos never leave your iPhone
  • Encryption in transit for analytics data
  • Regular security updates and patches
  • Limited server-side data retention

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Privacy Rights

General Users

You can:

  • Revoke photo library access anytime through iOS Settings
  • Disable personalized ads in iOS Settings
  • Delete the app to stop data collection
  • Request information about data we process

European Union (GDPR)

If you're in the EEA or UK, you have additional rights:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Right to Deletion: Request deletion of your data
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Right to Portability: Receive your data in portable format
  • Right to Object: Object to processing

California (CCPA/CPRA)

California residents have rights including:

  • Right to Know: What personal information is collected
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of collected data
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt out of sale/sharing of personal information
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: No discrimination for exercising rights

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will promptly delete such information.

8. Data Retention

  • Photos: Never stored on our servers
  • Usage Analytics: Retained for up to 60 days
  • Crash Reports: Retained for 90 days
  • Ad Data: Governed by Google's retention policies

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or applicable laws. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy and posting the new version on this page.

Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
