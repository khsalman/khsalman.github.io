Core features

Everything you need to stay committed

Simple enough to use every day. Powerful enough to drive real change.

✅

Daily check-ins

Quick tap-based interface. Mark commitments as Done, Missed, or Skip. Undo anytime with a 5-second grace period.

🔥

Streak tracking

Current, longest, and personal best streaks per commitment. Earn Streak Savers every 7 days, and use Vacation Mode to pause without penalties.

🤝

Partner accountability

Invite friends via unique VOW-XXXXXX codes. Share challenges, a live leaderboard, and medal badges to keep each other honest.

Beautiful analytics

Success rate charts, streak longevity rankings, best time-of-day heatmaps, and a year-in-review summary — all on-device.

Apple Watch app

Standalone watchOS app with today's commitments, haptic feedback, and 5 complication families to keep your streak front and center.

Privacy-first

No backend servers. All data stored locally by default. Optional CloudKit sync is end-to-end encrypted — Apple can't read it either.

How it works

From intention to habit in three steps

Create a commitment

Name your goal, set a frequency (daily to monthly), add a reminder, and optionally set an end date or difficulty level.

Check in daily

One tap — Done, Missed, or Skip. Your streak updates instantly. Miss one? Use a Streak Saver or trigger Vacation Mode to stay protected.

See yourself grow

Watch streaks climb, explore analytics, share progress cards, and keep partners honest with challenges and leaderboards.
