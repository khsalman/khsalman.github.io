LynxApps/Vow/Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Your commitment data never leaves your device by default. Vow stores all check-ins, streaks, and commitment data locally using encrypted on-device storage. No backend servers. No account required. CloudKit sync is optional, end-to-end encrypted, and user-controlled.

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Vow ("App"), developed by LynxApps ("we," "us," "our"), handles your information. Vow is a native iOS commitment tracking and accountability application. We believe your personal habit data is yours — it is stored locally by default and never transmitted to our servers.

By using Vow, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information Stored On Your Device

The following data is created and stored entirely on your device using SwiftData with device encryption. It is never uploaded to our servers:

  • Commitment names, descriptions, frequencies, and end dates
  • Daily check-in history (Done / Missed / Skip)
  • Streak data (current, longest, personal best)
  • Streak Saver balances and Vacation Mode periods
  • Analytics data (success rates, heatmaps, year-in-review)
  • App preferences (theme, accent colour, font size, reminder times)
  • Partner connection codes and local partnership data

3. Optional CloudKit Sync

If you choose to enable cross-device sync (Settings → Data → Enable Sync), your data is synced via Apple's CloudKit private database:

  • Data is end-to-end encrypted — Apple cannot read your data
  • Sync uses your personal iCloud account and Apple's private CloudKit container
  • LynxApps has no access to your synced data
  • You can disable sync and delete all iCloud copies at any time via Settings → Data

4. Information Collected Automatically

To keep the App stable and continuously improve it, limited anonymous technical data is collected via trusted third-party services. This never includes your personal commitment or check-in data.

  • Firebase Analytics: anonymous app usage events (features used, session duration, app version)
  • Firebase Crashlytics: anonymous crash reports and error logs to fix bugs
  • Device information: iOS version, device model, locale (for analytics only)

Vow does not use Google AdMob or serve advertisements of any kind.

5. Partner Accountability System

The partner system uses WCSession (WatchConnectivity) for local device communication. When connecting with an accountability partner:

  • Invitations use unique VOW-XXXXXX codes generated on-device
  • Shared data is limited to streak counts and challenge progress — not commitment names or check-in details unless you choose to share them
  • Partner connections can be removed at any time from Settings

6. Apple Watch

The Apple Watch app communicates with the iPhone app via WatchConnectivity. No data is transmitted outside your personal device ecosystem.

7. Calendar Integration

If you enable Calendar sync (Premium), the App uses Apple's EventKit framework to write recurring events to your local iOS Calendar. We request only the minimum permissions required. Calendar data stays on your device and is managed by Apple.

8. Siri Shortcuts

Siri Shortcuts integration uses Apple's SiriKit framework entirely on-device. No commitment data is transmitted to Apple's servers through this feature.

9. In-App Purchases

All purchases are processed through Apple's App Store via StoreKit 2. We do not collect or store payment card information. We receive only purchase confirmation, subscription status, and receipt information from Apple.

11. Your Rights & Controls

  • Delete all data: use Settings → Data → Delete All Data, or uninstall the App
  • Disable CloudKit sync: Settings → Data → Disable Sync and delete iCloud copy
  • Manage notifications: iOS Settings → Notifications → Vow
  • Export your data: Settings → Export Data (CSV or PDF — Premium feature)
  • Remove a partner: Settings → Partners → Remove

GDPR (European Union / EEA)

You have the right to access, correct, delete, and port your data, as well as object to processing. To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below.

CCPA / CPRA (California)

You have the right to know what data is collected, request deletion, and opt out of sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information.

12. Data Security

  • On-device data uses SwiftData with device-level encryption
  • Optional CloudKit sync is end-to-end encrypted
  • All analytics data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS
  • No tracking cookies or third-party advertising SDKs
  • User-initiated data deletion is immediate and complete

13. Children's Privacy

Vow is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

14. Data Retention

  • Commitment & check-in data: stored locally on your device until you delete it
  • CloudKit data: synced until you disable sync and delete iCloud copy
  • Analytics data: retained for up to 60 days in anonymised form
  • Crash reports: retained for up to 90 days

15. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
