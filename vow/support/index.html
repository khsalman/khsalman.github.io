Getting started

How do I create my first commitment?+
  1. Tap the + button on the main screen
  2. Give your commitment a name (e.g., "Exercise 30 mins")
  3. Choose your frequency: daily, weekly, or monthly
  4. Optionally set a reminder time, difficulty level, or end date
  5. Tap Save — your commitment is ready to check into
How do check-ins work?+

Each day, tap a commitment to open the check-in screen. Choose Done ✅, Missed ❌, or Skip ⏭. Done and Skip keep your streak alive; Missed breaks it. You have a 5-second undo window after any check-in if you tap by mistake.

What are Streak Savers?+

Streak Savers are your safety net. You earn one Saver every 7 days at a streak milestone. If you miss a day, you can spend a Saver to protect your streak instead of resetting it. Check your Saver balance on the commitment detail screen.

What is Vacation Mode?+

Vacation Mode lets you pause a commitment for 7 to 14 days without resetting your streak. Open the commitment, tap ⋯ → Vacation Mode, and choose your pause duration. Perfect for holidays, travel, or planned breaks.

How do commitment end dates work?+

When you set an end date, Vow celebrates your completion with a dialog showing your final stats when the date arrives. You can then choose to renew the commitment (new streak) or archive it. Great for 30-day challenges, courses, or seasonal goals.

Streaks & progress

What counts as a streak?+

A streak increments every time you mark a commitment as Done or Skip on the scheduled day. Marking it as Missed resets the streak to zero (unless you use a Streak Saver). The app tracks three streak values per commitment: current, longest ever, and personal best.

Can I check in more than once per day?+

Yes — for commitments like "Drink 8 glasses of water," you can set a multi-check-in target and log each one separately. The progress bar fills as you complete each check-in, and the day is counted as Done when you hit your target.

How do I view my analytics?+

Go to Insights in the tab bar (Premium). You'll see success rate by category, streak longevity rankings, a 24-hour best time-of-day heatmap, and a year-in-review summary. All data is computed on-device — no uploads needed.

How do I share my streak?+

On any commitment detail screen, tap the Share button. Vow generates a beautiful streak card with your branding and a caption with #VowApp. Share directly to Instagram Stories, Twitter, Messages, or anywhere via the system share sheet.

Premium & subscriptions

What's included in Premium?+

Premium unlocks:

  • Apple Watch standalone app with complications
  • Calendar integration (sync commitments to iOS Calendar)
  • Difficulty levels per commitment (Easy / Medium / Hard)
  • Full analytics dashboard
  • Data export (CSV and PDF)
  • Partner accountability system with challenges and leaderboard
  • Cross-device CloudKit sync (iPhone, iPad, Mac)
  • Family Sharing for up to 6 members
How do I manage or cancel my subscription?+
  1. Open Settings on your iPhone
  2. Tap [Your Name] at the top
  3. Tap Subscriptions
  4. Select Vow
  5. Tap Cancel Subscription or change your plan
How does Family Sharing work?+

When you purchase Premium (Monthly or Annual), Family Sharing is automatically included. Up to 5 additional family members in your Apple Family Sharing group can use all Premium features at no extra charge. Each person's data remains separate and private on their own device.

How do I restore my purchase on a new device?+

Open Vow → Settings → Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase. Premium will be restored instantly.

Partner system & sync

How do I add an accountability partner?+
  1. Open Partners in the tab bar (Premium)
  2. Tap Invite Partner to generate your unique VOW-XXXXXX code
  3. Share the code with your partner
  4. They enter it in their Vow app under Partners → Join with Code
  5. Once connected, you'll see each other's streak leaderboard and can start shared challenges
What data is shared with my partner?+

Only your streak counts and challenge progress are shared by default — not commitment names or individual check-in details. You control what is visible. Connections can be removed at any time from Settings → Partners.

How does cross-device sync work?+

Enable CloudKit sync in Settings → Data → Enable Sync. Your data syncs across iPhone, iPad, and Mac using Apple's private CloudKit database with end-to-end encryption. LynxApps has no access to your synced data. You can disable and delete the iCloud copy at any time.

Apple Watch & Siri

How do I use Vow on my Apple Watch?+

Install Vow from the App Store and the Watch app installs automatically. Open it from your Watch to see today's commitments. Tap a commitment, confirm with the 3-tap guard, and you'll feel haptic feedback confirming your check-in. Add a Vow complication to your watch face for instant access.

How do Siri Shortcuts work?+

Vow includes three built-in shortcuts:

  • Check in — mark a commitment as Done
  • Voice check-in — "Hey Siri, check in with Vow"
  • Show streaks — see your current streaks

Add them to your Lock Screen or Control Center via Settings → Siri & Search → Vow.

Troubleshooting

Notifications not arriving

  1. Go to Settings → Notifications → Vow and ensure notifications are allowed
  2. Confirm the reminder toggle is on for the specific commitment inside Vow
  3. Check that Focus / Do Not Disturb isn't silencing Vow
  4. Verify your device time zone is correct in Settings → General → Date & Time
  5. Restart the app or your iPhone if reminders still don't appear

Streak appears incorrect

  1. Check that your device time zone is set correctly
  2. Review your check-in history on the commitment detail screen
  3. Ensure you have Full Access to the commitment (no iCloud sync conflicts)
  4. If the issue persists, contact us with your device model and iOS version

CloudKit sync not working

  1. Go to Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud and check Vow is listed and enabled
  2. Check your internet connection
  3. Toggle sync off and on in Vow → Settings → Data
  4. Sign out of iCloud and back in if issues persist
