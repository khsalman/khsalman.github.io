Talk to Orvia the way you'd brief a great executive assistant — it turns intent into scheduled tasks, focus sessions, and reminders.

Home — "What now?"

Your command center. A greeting, the AI orb, today's prioritized tasks, your next event, and a quick Focus entry point — answers what to do next instantly.

✅

Tasks

Create, prioritize, and complete tasks. Orvia can generate and triage them from a single sentence, surfacing what matters today versus later.

🎯

Focus

Distraction-defending Focus sessions with a lock-screen Live Activity that keeps your timer visible and protected while your phone is away.

Goals

Longer-horizon objectives that connect down to daily action — goals link to the tasks and focus time that actually move them.

Calendar
