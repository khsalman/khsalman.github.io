Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Your inspection data stays on your device. All inspection data, photos, and reports are stored locally on your device. We never upload, transmit, or share your inspection data with external servers unless you explicitly enable iCloud sync.

1. Introduction

InspectKit Site Reports Pro ("we," "us," "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and otherwise handle your information when you use our mobile application, InspectKit (the "App").

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

  • Business information (company name, owner name, phone, email)
  • Client information (names, contact details for inspection purposes)
  • Property inspection data (locations, inspection details, findings)
  • Photos and annotations you capture within the App
  • Voice notes you record
  • Account information if you create an account
  • Payment information for subscription purchases (processed through Apple's App Store)

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device information (device type, operating system, unique device identifiers)
  • App usage data (features used, time spent, interactions)
  • Error logs and crash reports
  • Camera and microphone access logs

Information from iCloud

When you enable iCloud sync, we store your inspection data on your iCloud account for synchronization across your devices.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Providing and maintaining the App functionality
  • Processing subscription purchases
  • Generating inspection reports
  • Improving App features and user experience
  • Diagnosing technical issues
  • Complying with legal obligations
  • Communicating with you about updates or support

We do NOT:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your inspection data with external services without your consent
  • Use your information for marketing purposes beyond App-related communications
  • Collect information beyond what's necessary for App functionality

4. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

Your inspection data is stored locally on your device. You have full control over this data and can delete it at any time by uninstalling the App or clearing the App's data.

iCloud Sync

When enabled, inspection data syncs to your personal iCloud account. This data is encrypted and stored according to Apple's privacy standards. You control whether iCloud sync is enabled.

Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security practices to protect your information, including encryption and secure data transmission. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Data Retention

You control how long your data is retained. Delete inspections and reports directly from the App at any time.

5. Third-Party Services

Apple App Store

For subscription purchases and in-app billing. Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

iCloud

For optional data synchronization. Subject to Apple's iCloud Privacy Policy.

Analytics (if enabled)

Basic usage analytics to improve App performance. No personally identifiable information is shared.

6. Your Privacy Rights

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your information by uninstalling the App
  • Export your data in standard formats
  • Disable iCloud sync at any time
  • Opt out of analytics collection

To exercise these rights, contact us at lynxapps.contact@gmail.com
