How it works

Three steps to organized expenses

Scan

Snap a photo of any receipt or import one from your library. The app detects the edges for you.

Review

On-device OCR pulls out the merchant, amount, and date. Tweak anything in a tap if needed.

Understand

Your spending dashboard updates instantly so you always know where your money is going.
