LynxApps/PillVault/Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 29, 2026

Your medication data never leaves your device. PillVault stores everything locally. We do not require an account and we never transmit your medication information to our servers.

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how PillVault ("App"), developed by LynxApps ("we," "us," "our"), handles your information. PillVault is a personal medication tracking tool that lets you log medication names, dosages, schedules, refill dates, and dose history, and generates local reminders on your device.

By using PillVault, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

PillVault is built to be private by default. We do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or phone number
  • Your location or contacts
  • Your medication names, dosages, schedules, or dose history

Your medication data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to or accessed by us on any server.

3. Information Collected Automatically

To keep the App stable and improve it, limited, anonymous technical data is collected through trusted third-party services. This data never includes your medication contents.

  • Crash reports — anonymous diagnostics when the app encounters an error
  • Usage analytics — aggregate, anonymized information about which features are used
  • Advertising identifier — only if you grant permission via Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt

4. Third-Party Services

Google Firebase (Crashlytics & Analytics)

Collects anonymous crash reports and usage data to help us diagnose problems and improve the App. Medication contents are never included.

5. Local Notifications

PillVault generates dose and refill reminders as local notifications on your device. These are created and delivered entirely on-device and are not processed through any external server.

6. Your Choices & Controls

  • Delete your data anytime within the App, or by uninstalling PillVault
  • Disable notifications in iOS Settings → Notifications → PillVault
  • Manage ad tracking in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking
  • Reset your advertising identifier through iOS privacy settings

7. Children's Privacy

PillVault is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided information, please contact us and we will address it promptly.

8. Data Security

Because your medication data stays on your device, it benefits from your device's built-in security, including device encryption and passcode/Face ID protection. We encourage you to keep your device secured and updated.

9. Not a Medical Device

PillVault is a personal organizational tool. It is not a medical device and is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional regarding your medications.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

  • Response time: typically 2–3 business days
