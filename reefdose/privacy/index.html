Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Your tank data is encrypted and secure. All tank parameters, dosing records, and livestock information are stored locally on your device or encrypted in iCloud. We do not collect, analyze, or share your data with third parties.

1. Overview

LynxApps ("we," "us," "our") operates the ReefDose application ("App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use the App. We are committed to protecting your privacy — your reef tank data is encrypted and stays under your control.

2. Information We Collect

Tank & Parameter Data

The App stores reef tank information including tank name, volume, equipment details, water parameters (calcium, alkalinity, magnesium, pH, salinity, temperature), dosing records, livestock inventory, and maintenance logs.

Important: All data is stored locally on your device using SwiftData. With iCloud enabled, your data is encrypted and synced to CloudKit (Apple's private database). Your data is never sent to our servers.

Device & Usage Information

Through third-party services, we automatically collect:

  • Device model and operating system version
  • App usage data and feature interactions
  • Crash reports and error logs

In-App Purchase Information

All subscription purchases are processed through Apple's App Store. We do not collect or store payment card information. We receive only purchase confirmation and subscription status information.

3. How We Use Information

  • App functionality: to store and manage your reef tank data
  • iCloud sync: to encrypt and sync your data across your devices
  • Service improvement: to enhance features and user experience
  • Analytics: to understand usage patterns and improve performance
  • Support: to provide customer support and troubleshooting
  • Legal compliance: to comply with applicable laws

4. Data Storage & Encryption

Local Storage (Free Plan)

Tank data is stored locally on your device using SwiftData. Your data remains on your device unless you enable iCloud sync.

iCloud CloudKit (Pro Plan)

When you enable iCloud sync (Pro plan), your data is encrypted using Apple's CloudKit encryption and stored in Apple's private database. CloudKit encryption means:

  • Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Only you can decrypt your data (with your Apple ID credentials)
  • Apple cannot access your tank data

iCloud sync is optional — you can use ReefDose entirely offline without syncing.

6. Your Privacy Rights

General Users

  • Delete all your data by uninstalling the app or deleting from iCloud settings
  • Disable iCloud sync anytime in app settings
  • Request information about data we process

European Union (GDPR)

  • Access: request a copy of your data
  • Deletion: request deletion of your data
  • Rectification: correct inaccurate data
  • Portability: receive your data in a portable format
  • Object: object to processing

California (CCPA/CPRA)

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete collected data
  • Right to opt-out of sale/sharing of personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

Brazil (LGPD)

  • Right to access, correct, and delete your data
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to opt-out of data processing

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13, and will promptly delete such information if discovered.

8. Data Retention

  • Tank data: stored locally on your device; deleted when you uninstall the app or remove iCloud backup
  • Usage analytics: retained up to 60 days
  • Crash reports: retained up to 90 days
  • CloudKit data: synced to iCloud; you control deletion
